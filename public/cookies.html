Cookie Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Short version: We use essential cookies the app needs to work, plus two optional analytics tools (Google Analytics 4 and PostHog). You can accept or decline analytics.

What is a cookie?

A small text file stored on your device. We also use local storage and session tokens for the same purposes — the rules below apply equally to those.

Essential cookies (always on — no consent needed)

Name / type Purpose
Authentication / session token (Supabase) Keeps you logged in. Without this the app cannot work.
Security / CSRF tokens Protect your account from cross-site request forgery.
Local preferences (e.g. dark mode) Remember your settings between sessions.
Cookie consent choice (localStorage: jp.analytics_consent) Remembers whether you accepted or declined analytics cookies, so we don't ask again on every visit and don't load analytics before you've chosen.

Analytics cookies (require consent)

Service What it collects Used for
Google Analytics 4 (_ga, _ga_<id>) Pages visited, features used, device/browser type, approximate location (country/city). IP is anonymised by GA4 before storage. Cookie _ga expires after 2 years; _ga_<id> expires after 2 years. Understanding usage and improving the app. Not used to identify you personally or for advertising. Data is processed by Google LLC (USA) under Standard Contractual Clauses / the UK Addendum.
PostHog (ph_* localStorage keys) Screen views and feature interactions. Stored in localStorage (not a cookie). Data retained for 12 months. EU-hosted: eu.i.posthog.com. Session replay is not currently enabled. Product analytics and understanding feature adoption. Not used for advertising. Data is processed by PostHog Inc. on EU servers (no US transfer).

Analytics cookies are only set if you accept them via the cookie banner. The analytics toggle in Settings → Cookie settings reflects your stored choice: it stays off until you accept, and you can switch it off again at any time to withdraw consent.

Third-party cookies

Stripe may set cookies during payment flows, governed by Stripe's own privacy policy.

Change your cookie choice

If you're signed in, the quickest way to change your mind is in-app: Settings → Cookie settings, a single tap to switch analytics on or off.

If you're not signed in, or you'd rather reset it on this device, use the button below. It clears the saved choice so the cookie banner asks you again next time you open the app.

Managing cookies

Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Blocking essential cookies will break the app (you won't be able to log in). Blocking analytics cookies has no effect on app functionality.
