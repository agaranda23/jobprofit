1. Who we are

JobProfit is operated by JOB PROFIT LTD (company number 17249792), registered at 128 City Road, London, EC1V 2NX, United Kingdom. Contact: getjobprofit@gmail.com. We are registered with the ICO (registration number ZC163042).

2. The short version

We collect what we need to make the app work. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with anyone who does not need it to run the service. You can export everything or delete your account at any time — both are in the app.

3. What data we hold and why

3a. Data about you (the trader — account data): JobProfit is the data controller.

  • Name, email, phone — account creation, login, support, notifications (lawful basis: contract).
  • Business name, business address — populating quotes/invoices (contract).
  • Bank details (account name, sort code, account number) — displaying payment details on invoices (contract).
  • VAT number, UTR number — compliance and invoicing (contract/legal obligation).
  • Hourly rate — calculating job costs and profit (contract).
  • Logo — branding quotes/invoices (contract).
  • App usage data (pages, features, errors) — improving the product (legitimate interests; we use Google Analytics 4 and PostHog, see Section 5).

3b. Data about your customers (job data): When you quote/invoice/log jobs, YOU are the data controller and JobProfit is your data processor.

We process this only on your instruction. This includes customer name, address, phone, email, job descriptions, line items, amounts, and an e-signature (PNG) when a customer accepts a quote via a public link. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to process your customers' data and for responding to their data subject requests. A Data Processing Agreement is available on request at getjobprofit@gmail.com.

For all data about your customers, JobProfit acts only as your processor and only on your documented instructions: we use it to provide the service to you (storing your jobs, generating your quotes and invoices, and sending messages you ask us to send) and for nothing else. We do not use your customers' data for our own purposes, do not sell it, and do not use it to market to your customers. The one exception is that we may include this data, once fully anonymised and aggregated so it can no longer identify any individual, within the anonymised statistics described in Section 4a — at which point it is no longer personal data.

Payment reminders (WhatsApp and SMS): When you send a payment reminder by WhatsApp or SMS, JobProfit prepares a pre-filled message and opens it in your own WhatsApp or messaging app on your device — you send it yourself. We do not transmit your customer's phone number to WhatsApp/Meta, any SMS provider, or any other third party as part of this feature.

3c. Providing your data. Some of the account data above (such as your name, email, and business details) is necessary to provide the service under our contract with you — if you do not provide it, we may be unable to create or operate your account. Analytics data is optional and based on your consent.

3d. Automated decision-making. We do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

4. How long we keep data

  • Account data: kept while your account is active. When you delete your account, your account data is erased immediately and permanently — we do not keep it for a grace period. The only exceptions are the limited transaction records we are legally required to retain for accounting and tax (see the Stripe bullet below) and any anonymised, non-identifying statistics that no longer relate to you (see Section 4a).
  • Job and invoice records: kept for as long as your account is active, because they are your business records and you control them. We hold them as your processor on your instruction. (Separately, HMRC generally expects sole traders to keep their own financial records for around 6 years — that is your obligation as a business, not a rule we impose, and you can export your records at any time to meet it.)
  • Unconverted leads (quotes never accepted or invoiced): automatically removed about 6 months after the last activity on that job, or sooner if you delete them.
  • Analytics (Google Analytics 4): data is retained for 14 months (GA4 default) and kept no longer than necessary for product analytics.
  • Analytics (PostHog): data is retained for 12 months (PostHog EU cloud default) and kept no longer than necessary for product analytics.
  • Stripe transaction records: Stripe retains per its policies; we retain metadata up to 7 years for accounting/tax.

4a. Anonymised and aggregated data

We may create anonymised and aggregated statistics from activity in JobProfit — for example, typical job values, average time-to-payment, or how common a type of work is across our users. Once data has been aggregated and stripped of anything that could identify you or your customers, it is no longer personal data, and we may keep and use it indefinitely to operate, improve and build JobProfit's products and insights. This data cannot be traced back to you, and deleting your account does not delete these anonymised statistics because they no longer contain your information.

4b. Deleting your data

You can delete your account at any time from Settings, and you can delete individual jobs, quotes and invoices as you go. When you delete your account, we erase your personal data and your customers' data immediately and permanently from our live systems — there is no recovery period, so please export anything you need first. Two narrow things survive deletion: (1) limited transaction records our payment processor and UK tax law require us to keep (see the Stripe bullet in Section 4), and (2) anonymised, non-identifying statistics that no longer relate to you (see Section 4a). Backups are overwritten on our provider's normal rolling cycle.

4c. Inactive accounts

If you stop using JobProfit without deleting your account, we keep your data for the life of your active account and then for a further period of inactivity of 36 months. After that period of no logins and no activity, we will email you to let you know, and unless you reactivate we will then delete or fully anonymise your account and its data. The only exception is the limited transaction metadata we must keep for accounting and tax (see Section 4). This means we do not hold personal data indefinitely just because an account was abandoned.

5. Who we share data with (sub-processors)

  • Supabase — database hosting — all account and job data — hosted in the EU (eu-west-1, Ireland / EEA). The EEA holds UK adequacy status, so no additional transfer safeguards (IDTA/SCCs) are required for this data. (Supabase Inc. is US-incorporated; a Data Processing Agreement with SCCs/UK Addendum is in place to cover any access from the US.)
  • Netlify — hosting/serverless — request metadata, logs — USA; transfers covered by Standard Contractual Clauses / the UK Addendum (per Netlify's Data Processing Agreement).
  • Stripe / Stripe Connect — payment processing and trader payment-account linking — trader name, email, billing metadata; card/bank-card details held directly by Stripe, JobProfit never sees or stores them — USA (EU-US DPF).
  • Supabase Auth (via Supabase's infrastructure) — delivery of transactional email (login links and account notifications) — recipient email address and message content — hosted on Supabase's EU infrastructure; no separate third-party email provider is currently used.
  • Twilio (Twilio Inc.) — SMS delivery for login verification codes — recipient phone numbers and message content — USA; transfers covered by Standard Contractual Clauses / the UK Addendum (per Twilio's Data Processing Addendum).
  • Google Analytics 4 (Google LLC) — pseudonymised usage events and device metadata; cookies set: _ga, _ga_<id>. Data is processed on Google's servers (USA); transfers are covered by the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses / the UK Addendum. IP anonymisation is enabled by default in GA4. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.
  • PostHog (PostHog Inc.) — pseudonymised usage events and session analytics; data is stored on PostHog's EU cloud (eu.i.posthog.com, hosted in the EU). No cross-site tracking; no advertising cookies. Session replay is not currently enabled. PostHog's privacy policy: posthog.com/privacy.
  • Anthropic — AI quote generation — job descriptions and pricing history (no customer contact details) — USA; transfers covered by Standard Contractual Clauses / the UK Addendum.

We do not sell data or use it for advertising targeting.

6. International transfers

Where data is transferred outside the UK we rely on UK adequacy regulations, the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA), or the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, as applicable.

7. Your rights (UK GDPR)

Access, rectify, erase, restrict/object, data portability, withdraw consent, lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).

In-app: export all data as CSV from Settings; delete your account from Settings. For anything beyond the in-app tools, or if you are a customer of a tradesperson who uses JobProfit, contact getjobprofit@gmail.com — for tradespeople's customers we act as processor and will direct your request to the relevant tradesperson (the controller).

8. Security

HTTPS/TLS, access controls, Supabase Row Level Security. No system is fully secure. If a personal data breach occurs that is likely to result in a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will report it to the ICO without undue delay and, where feasible, within 72 hours of becoming aware of it (UK GDPR Article 33), and we will tell you without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to you (Article 34).

9. Changes

Material changes notified by email or in-app before they take effect; the "last updated" date reflects the current version.
